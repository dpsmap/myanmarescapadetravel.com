Welcome to Myanmar!

Myanmar, formerly known as (Burma), is known as the Golden Land. It is also called the Land of Pagodas. Myanmar is one of the most devout Buddhist countries, and has long been a unique tourist destination in the South East Asia and Pacific, for its rich religious and cultural heritage sites. A diverse nation with 135 distinct ethnic groups, are grouped into eight major national ethnic races - Kachin, Kayah, Kayin, Chin, Bamar, Mon, Rakhine and Shan. Nay Pyi Taw meaning “The Royal City” is the capital of Myanmar.

A few of our top destinations are: Yangon, Mandalay, Bagan, Inle Lake, Mt. Kyaiktiyo (Golden Rock Pagoda), Mrauk U, and the Ngapali Beach. You can check out our list of package tours that’s right for you. In addition, we would like to introduce you to our new emerging tourism sites and destinations, including Putao, the Myeik Archipelago, Nagaland, Hakha, Nat Ma Taung, and Loikaw.
