Day 01: Arrival Yangon (L/D)

International arrival to Yangon. After check-in to the hotel, visit the most enchanting and spectacular Pagoda of Myanmar the Shwedagon and Sule Pagoda. Lunch at a local restaurant. Evening at your own leisure and enjoy the sunset over the Pagodas. Dinner at a local restaurant and stay overnight at your hotel in Yangon.

Day 02: Yangon – Sittwe (by flight) – Cruising to Mrauk U (B/L/D)

After breakfast, you will be transferred to Yangon airport for domestic flight to Sittwe. Arrive at Sittwe transfer to the Jetty where you board your private boat to Mrauk U. Packed lunch is included during boat trip.
The journey on the Kaladan River, through changing scenery, upriver towards the hill surrounding Mrauk U. Mrauk U, was Rakhine's capital in 15th century, when it enjoyed great wealth and was an important commercial port. Marauk U has great charm with is many local markets, solid wooden house and Bamboo houses, canals, hills and friendly people. Visit some of the ancient pagodas and ruins by foot or a horse cart, bullock cart or vehicle from the town. Then, transfer to Hotel for check –in. Start your sightseeing at Mrauk U. Visit Shittaung Pagoda, Andaw Pagoda, Dukkanthein "ordination hall", a construction of late rite and sandstone bricks that resembles a huge bunker from the outside. Enjoy sunset at Harre Taund Pagoda Hill. Dinner at local restaurant and overnight stay at your hotel, in Mrauk-U.

Day 03: Mrauk U – Chin Tatto Village (B/L/D)

After breakfast at hotel, visit Mrauk U's Main Market and Koe Thaung Temple. Continue to Lay Myo river bank. And then proceed by boat to Chin Tattoo area. We are trail to three Chin Tattoo Villages by boat and walk to the hill tribe's people. Visit to waving project at the chin village. The villagers are very basic life style. Some Tattoo ladies are very proud of their tattoo designed on their faces and waving designed. You will see how the villagers will warmly welcome and friendly. Packed lunch is included. Return back to Mrauk U and enjoy your dinner at a local restaurant. Overnight stay at your hotel.

Day 04: Mrauk U – Sittwe – Yangon (B/L/D)

After an early morning breakfast, return back to Sittwe by your private boat. Visit Archaeology Museum, Palace Site. Transfer to Jetty where you board your private speed boat to Sittwe. The journey on the Kaladan River, through changing scenery, downriver towards the sea and Island, surrounding Sittwe. Upon arrival, transfer to Fish Market, Cultural Museum. Lunch at local restaurant. In the afternoon, transfer to Sittwe Airport for your flight back to Yangon. Arrival at Yangon, transfer to your hotel for check-in. Enjoy your dinner and overnight stay at your hotel in Yangon.

Day 05: Yangon – Departure (B/L)

After breakfast, the day ends with time for free and easy shopping adventures at Bogyoke Scott Market to browse and bargain freely among the hundreds of stalls for great souvenirs. Savor bargains delights and thrills of shopping among the many stalls and hawkers along the streets of Chinatown. After lunch, you will be transferred to the airport for departure.
