Day 1: Arrival Yangon

Arrive at Yangon Airport and then transfer to hotel. Proceed to Yangon City Golf Course for 18 holes golf. After golf, transfer back to the hotel. Late evening visit Shwedagon Pagoda- world famous golden stupa in the dome shape covered with 60 tons of pure gold. Enjoy dinner at local restaurant. Stay overnight in hotel.

Day 2: Yangon – Heho – Inle

After breakfast, transfers to the airport for flight to Heho. Visit by boat in Inle Lake includes observing unique leg-rowers, fishing canoes, floating gardens and plantations. Then visit well known Nga Pe Chaung (Jumping Cat) monastery. Afternoon visit includes: Cheroot-Making Factory; Silk and Cotton-Weaving Industry. In late afternoon visit famous Phaung Daw Oo Pagoda. Enjoy your dinner and an overnight stay at your hotel.

Day 3: Taungyi Golf

After breakfast, transfer to Aye Tharyar Golf Course for 18 holes. After lunch take your own leisure time. Enjoy your night life with a massage, designed to provide you with the choice of pure relaxation therapy or specialized remedial treatments for soft tissue injuries and related conditions. Overnight stay at hotel.

Day 4: Taungyi-Yangon-Home

After breakfast you will be transferred to the airport for domestic flight to Yangon. Upon arrival visit to Bogyoke Market, the largest market in Yangon with time to browse among the hundreds of stalls for souvenir bargains, arts & crafts souvenir shopping center. After lunch at a local restaurant, transfer to Yangon International Airport for departure.
