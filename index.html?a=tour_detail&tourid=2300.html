Day 01: Yangon Arrival

Welcome at Yangon Airport. Transfer to hotel for check-in. Take your own leisure time. Stay overnight at your hotel.

Day 02: Yangon Golf

After breakfast, transfer from the hotel to the Pan Hlaing Golf Course for 18 holes. After you have been playing the golfing, return back to hotel and rest for a while. An evening visit to Myanmar's most revered Shwedagon Pagoda - 2500 years' old world famous Golden Stupa. Then, savour bargains delights and thrills of shopping among the many stalls and hawkers along the streets of Chinatown. Stay overnight at your hotel.

Day 03: Yangon – Bagan

After breakfast, you will be transferred to the Yangon Airport for your flight to Bagan. After check in your hotel, take a trip around the city by river to see some of its famous temples and stupas, including Shwezigon stupa, Gubyaukkyi temple, Lawkahtaikpan temple, Ananda temple and the massive Dhammayangyi temple. After lunch, take your own leisure time. Have dinner with Myanmar Traditional Show and overnight rest in your hotel.

Day 04: Bagan Golf

Early breakfast, followed by an 18 round of golf at Bagan Golf Course, opened 1996 and host of International London Golf Tournaments. This golf course is especially beautiful located right in the heart of Old Bagan with its century old pagodas and temples. Enjoy Sunset from a temple in the evening. Stay overnight in hotel.

Day 05: Bagan – MT. Popa – Bagan

Start your day watching the sunrise at Mee Nyein Gone Temple (The name "Mee Nyein Gone" means "the hill where the fire extinguishers". Return by horse carriage to the hotel for breakfast. Then, take a car trip to Mount Poppa, an extinct volcano claimed to be abode of Nat spirits. On this mountaintop are the pagodas, shrines and monasteries. Return to Began and visit the lacquer ware industry. Stay overnight at your hotel.

Day 06: Bagan – Mandalay Golf

After breakfast, flight to Mandalay and transfer for hotel check-in. Continue to play golf at Shwe Man Taung Golf Course. Afternoon, visit to Mandalay Palace, Mahamuni Pagoda , Gold Leaf Beating Workshop, Silk Weaving Home Cottage and Mandalay Hill to watch the sunset and the panoramic view of the city. Stay overnight at your hotel.

Day 07: Mandalay - Mingun

After breakfast, take (11 km; about 1 hour) a trip on a private boat up the Ayeyarwady River to visit Mingun, a village on the western bank. Tour highlights: very huge but unfinished Pahtodawgyi Pagoda; Mingun Bell, the world's largest undamaged ringing bell, which weighs 90 tons; Myatheintan Pagoda with seven concentric terraces. Return back to Mandalay. In the afternoon, have your free time for relaxation and own exploration. Stay overnight at your hotel.

Day 08: Mandalay Golf

After breakfast, transfer to play golf at Yay Ta Gun Taung Golf Course for 18 holes. After Lunch, visit 200-year-old U Bein’s teak bridge and gold-foil cottage industry. Stay overnight at your hotel in Mandalay.

Day 09 : Mandalay - Yangon

After breakfast, take domestic flight to Yangon. After lunch, visit the Bogyoke (Scott's) Market. Stay overnight at your hotel.

Day 10: Yangon - Home

After breakfast, you will be transferred to Yangon International Airport for departure flight.
