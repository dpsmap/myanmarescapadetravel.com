Loikaw

Loikaw is the capital of Kayah State in Myanmar. It is located in the Karen Hills area, near the State's northern tip, just above an embayment on the Pilu River. The inhabitants are mostly Kayah.
About Us

Myanmar Escapade Travel & Tours Co. Ltd. is a travel and tour operator in Myanmar and a full service travel agency handling both inbound and outbound tours. We are a registered privately-owned Myanmar company founded by a group of Directors who have extensive experience in various capacities within the hospitality industry. Myanmar Escapade Travel & Tours Co. Ltd. was officially launched in 2012.
